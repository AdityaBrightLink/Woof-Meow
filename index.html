About US

At Woof & Meow , we provide top-quality care for your pets. Our skilled vets and groomers ensure your furry friends receive the love, attention, and expertise they deserve for a happy, healthy life.

Our Founders

Dr. Prajakta Chetan Pawar

Founder & Veterinary Gynaecologist & Surgeon

With a passion for animals and a deep commitment to their health and well-being, Dr. Prajakta founded Woof & Meow Pet Clinic to provide compassionate and expert care for pets.

Mr. Pranav Jalinder Tumbare

Co-Founder

Pranav co-founded Woof & Meow with a vision to create a trusted space for pet care and grooming. His dedication ensures every aspect of our clinic runs smoothly, creating a comfortable experience for both pets and their owners.
